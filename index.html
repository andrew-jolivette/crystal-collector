Gem Collector!

Help the dwarven miners reach their quota of gems they need excavated. The value of the gems that need to be mined up changes every month based on discussions between both the Dwarven Commerce Committee and the Dwarven Resource Conservation League. So please be aware of the total value of gems you may mine up.

If that wasn't enough of a limitation, the Dwarven Commerce Committee seem to value the each type of crystal differently from month to month, supply and demand they say! To make matters even worse, you won't even know how much each type of crystal is worth until you mine one up.

Do you best and good luck!
